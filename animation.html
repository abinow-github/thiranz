Animation is the process of creating the illusion of motion through a rapid display of images, frames, or objects. It brings static elements to life, typically in film, video, or digital media, by simulating movement and progression over time.With our Animation Training in Chennai, students can enhance their artistic skills, discover their imaginative potential, and start a successful career in animation.
